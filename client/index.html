How Hive Wars plays

Phase 1: Build & Grow

Design your colony's layout strategically. Harvest sugar, train workers, and fortify your defenses. Your building placement determines your success in both defense and offense.

Phase 2: Scout & Plan

Analyze rival colonies. Identify weak points in their defenses. Plan attack routes and unit compositions. A good strategy beats brute force every time.

Phase 3: Raid & Conquer

Launch coordinated attacks on enemy hives. Deploy units strategically and guide them with pheromone trails. Loot resources and secure your position on the leaderboard.

Phase 4: Defend & Adapt

Rivals will attack your colony. Learn from each raid. Adjust your defenses. Upgrade your warriors. The strongest colonies are those that evolve.
